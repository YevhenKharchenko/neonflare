THE STORY BEGINS

Royal Adventure is more than a puzzle game — it’s a journey.

Join King William, Princess Nova, and Butler Nathan as they soar across the world in a magnificent hot-air balloon. From lush tropical rainforests to frozen tundras, every destination brings new challenges, surprises, and stories.

Each level you solve pushes the adventure forward. Each journey reveals a new chapter of a royally styled tale filled with wonder and discovery. Think regular match-3, but with actual reasons to keep playing.

GAMEPLAY WITH A TWIST

Royal Adventure takes familiar match-3 mechanics and layers them with depth:

  • Multi-layered puzzle boards

  • Obstacle-filled challenges that evolve as you play

  • Covering mechanics, rotating elements, and environmental hazards

ROYAL TOOLS & POWER MOVES

When you finally crack those tough levels, it feels pretty damn good.
The game rewards planning — not rushing.

When the path gets blocked, royal power steps in.

  • Laser beams to slice through obstacles

  • Royal cannons to clear dangerous zones

  • Special boosters created by smart matches

JOURNEY MAP & GLOBAL EXPLORATION

Every 50 levels, the world opens up.

  • Animated journey maps unlock new destinations

  • Story cutscenes bring the adventure to life

  • Each location introduces unique visuals and puzzle themes

You’re not just completing levels — you’re traveling the globe.

PLAY YOUR WAY (RELAXED BUT COMPETITIVE)

Perfect for chilling out, but you can still compete if that's your thing.

  • Easy to start, relaxing to play

  • No pressure gameplay for casual sessions

  • Optional competitive elements for those who want more

Compete on global leaderboards, challenge top players, and see how far your royal skills can take you.
